Toy Router

Get - /toys?page=1 - get list of all toys, present 10 toys per page
Get - /toys/search?page=1&s=sport - get list of toys, search by toy name or by toy info, present 10 toys per page
Get - /toys/cat/boardGames - get list of toys by category name, present 10 toys per page
Get - /toys/prices?min=40&max=600 - get list of toys by price range, present 10 toys per page
